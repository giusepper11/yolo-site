## Why

Create a polished, high-signal personal website that presents my professional story clearly and credibly while projecting a distinctive “enterprise meets edgy” brand. This will serve as a durable landing page to share with recruiters, collaborators, and future clients, and as a foundation for adding a portfolio later.

## What Changes

- Add a local-running Next.js website with a modern, responsive UI and a cohesive visual system (“enterprise meets edgy”).
- Include core content derived from `Profile.pdf`: About, Career Journey (timeline/roles), Skills/Tech Stack, and Contact/Social.
- Add a Portfolio section designed for future expansion (placeholders + structure, links ready).
- Add baseline SEO + social sharing metadata (OpenGraph), accessible navigation, and performance-focused defaults.
- Provide simple local developer workflow (install, run, lint/build) and a clear content-editing path.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `site-foundation`: Next.js app foundation (routing, layout, responsive shell, typography, theming).
- `brand-visual-system`: “enterprise meets edgy” design tokens (colors, type scale, spacing, components) and motion guidelines.
- `about-and-hero`: Hero + About content sections based on the LinkedIn profile summary and key highlights.
- `career-journey`: Experience timeline and role details (Glofox, Juvo, AMcom, FAPESP, TPS) with clear narrative progression.
- `skills-and-credentials`: Skills, tools, languages, certifications, and education blocks curated from `Profile.pdf`.
- `portfolio-placeholder`: A future-proof portfolio area with project cards/placeholders and external link affordances.
- `contact-and-social`: Contact section with LinkedIn/GitHub/email/phone, plus optional “copy to clipboard” actions.
- `seo-and-accessibility`: SEO metadata, sitemap/robots as appropriate, accessible semantics, and basic analytics hook point (optional).
